Project information

Upload photographs of affected spaces

You must provide photographs of any affected spaces within the school.

This can help us justify any higher costs, especially if the space is in poor condition.

Your photographs must be in PNG, JPG, or JPEG format, or you can upload the photographs collectively in a ZIP file.

Your file must be smaller than 20MB.

If you cannot reduce the size, email the photos and your unique reference number (URN) to images.schoolnurseries@education.gov.uk
