Project Overview

Upload floor and site plans

You must upload:

  • an existing floor plan with any marked changes
  • a proposed floor plan
  • a site plan showing the full school grounds

You should detail the areas where work will be carried out and the school is legally responsible for managing.

These plans will help assess if the planned works match the number of new places being provided.

Your file must be in PDF format and smaller than 20MB.
