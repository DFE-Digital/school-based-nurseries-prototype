Eligibility

Declaration

Before completing an application, you must first complete an eligibility check to confirm that your project meets all the eligibility criteria listed in the supporting guidance. These criteria have been designed to ensure the department upholds its responsibility to manage public money correctly.

Failure against any one of the eligibility criteria will result in automatic disqualification, and you will not be able to proceed to the full application stage.

If your project does not meet the eligibility criteria but you are interested in opening a nursery, you can register your interest. For more information, see the supporting guidance (opens in a new tab).
