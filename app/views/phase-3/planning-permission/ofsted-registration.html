Planning permission, significant change and childcare registration

What childcare registration action will the nursery need?

Schools, PVIs and providers delivering nursery places on a Best Start Family Hub site must register their nursery provision with Ofsted as a separate setting, unless they’re exempt. You should only start registration when you’re sure the new nursery places will open (for example, after receiving grant funding). Registration can take up to 12 weeks, so start it as soon as you can. Childminders may also need to register with Ofsted or with a childminder agency. Providers with an existing registration may need to update it before opening.
