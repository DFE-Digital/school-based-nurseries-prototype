Information you will need to provide

During the application process, you will need to provide:

  • details of any childerminders or Private, Voluntary, and Independent (PVI) partners, if relevant
  • evidence of how local childcare needs have been considered
  • evidence of your approach to early years education
  • feasibility and sustainability details
  • financial suitability details
  • full project cost breakdown
  • funding needed from addtional sources (if relevant)
  • risks associated with the project and key milestones
