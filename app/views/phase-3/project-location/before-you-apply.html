Eligibility

Before you apply

During the application process, you will need to provide:

  • details of any childerminders or Private, Voluntary, and Independent (PVI) partners, if relevant
  • information about the current or planned capacity for the term-time and holiday childminding
  • feasibility and sustainability details of the proposed nursery provision, including financial suitability
  • full project cost breakdowns
  • information on any additional funding needed
  • any risk associated with the project and key milestones
  • evidence showing how local childcare needs have been considered and your approach to Early Years education
  • mandatory documents: a marked-up floor plan, photos of the space for the capital works, and at least one cost breakdown from a qualified cost manager

You can find more information in our guidance.
