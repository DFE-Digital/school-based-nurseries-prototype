Phase 3 grant applications are open

Local authorities understand where the nursery provision is most needed in their area. This is why all local authorities in England are invited to apply for phase 3 of the School-based nurseries capital grant 2027 to 2030 (link opens in new tab).

! Warning Applications close at 5pm on Wednesday 30 September 2026

Each school-based nurseries capital grant:

  • be a local authority in England
  • have a DfE Sign-in account (link opens in new tab)
  • have read the guidance School-based nurseries capital grant 2027 to 2030 (link opens in new tab)

Individual settings can not apply for the phase 3 grant. Settings must ask their local authority Planning, Place and Provision (PPP) team or Early Years (EY) team lead to apply on their behalf. Before contacting the local authority, read the guidance on establishing school-based nursery provision (link opens in new tab).
