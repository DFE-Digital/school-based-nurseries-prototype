Confirm that your application is eligible

You need to confirm that your application meets the 5 eligibility criteria for a School-based nursery capital grant. These criteria help to ensure that the Department for Education upholds its responsibilty to manage public money correctly.

For information about these eligibility criteria, read the guidance on [get correct link name] (link opens in new tab)

  1. I am applying for a School-based nursery capital grant on behalf of a local authority.
  2. The new or extended school-based nursery will result in an increased number of childcare places. This increase takes into account any existing provision on site or nearby, including that delivered by external providers. 
  3. The new or extended school-based nursery will be operational and offer new childcare places within 3 years of receiving the grant.
  4. The new or extended school-based nursery is located on either:
    • a primary school site that already offers Early Years education
    • a maintained nursery school site 
  5. I have written permission from the relevant freehold landowner to use the land for nursery provision and carry out the proposed works. Example freehold landowners include the local authority, foundation or trust, or religious body.
If your project does not meet the eligibility criteria but you are interested in opening a nursery, you can register your interest. For more information, see the supporting guidance (opens in a new tab).
