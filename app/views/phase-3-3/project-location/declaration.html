Check eligibility of the proposed project

The proposed project must meet all 5 of the following criteria:

  1. The proposed project will result in a net increase in the number of childcare places, taking into account any existing provisions on site or nearby, including that delivered by external providers.
  2. The nursery will be operational and offering new childcare places by September 2030 at the latest.
  3. There is written permission, such as an email or a letter, from the freehold landowner to use the land and carry out the proposed works. Examples of freehold landowners include the local authority, a foundation or trust, or a religious body.
  4. The proposed project must be one of these types:
    • an school-based nursery (SBN) at a state-funded primary school in England that already offers early education, such as reception classes
    • part of a maintained nursery school (MNS) site in England
    • a Best Start Family Hub (BSFH) that already exists or is in the process of being set up - it can be on a school site or elsewhere
  5. If the proposed school received funding through the SBN capital grant funding in 2024-2025 or 2025-2026, the proposed project will add new places beyond what was delivered in earlier phases and offers good value for money.
